üìñ How It Works

For Administrators:

  1. Access the Admin Panel to manage your gift exchange
  2. Add participants individually or use bulk import
  3. Set gift value rules and guidelines
  4. Generate the draw once all participants are added
  5. Share unique links with each participant
  6. Monitor progress with the statistics dashboard

For Participants:

  1. Receive your unique link from the organizer
  2. Click the link to open your personalized reveal page
  3. Click "Reveal Your Secret Partner" button
  4. Enjoy the confetti animation and see your assignment
  5. View gift guidelines if provided
  6. Keep it secret until gift exchange day!
